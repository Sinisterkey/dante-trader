# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the AI Trading Agent community will be treated with respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome newcomers and help them
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Be patient and understanding

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, identity, or beliefs
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Threats or violence
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Spam or advertising

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Violations can result in temporary or permanent removal from the community.

## Reporting

If you witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the project maintainers.
